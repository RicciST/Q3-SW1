Unrequited Love


SYNOPSIS:

Unrequited Love follows the college student Luo Zhi as she balances college, her personal life, and her one-sided pining for her former high school batchmate Sheng Huainan, who she now attends college together with. For years, Luo Zhi had to put on an act and keep her interest in him a secret. The lies she made up for this act finally start to catch up with her. However, Unrequited Love ends on a sweet and heartwarming note.



Major Characters:

Luo Zhi
Sheng Huainan
Zhang Mingrui
Luo Zhi is an intelligent and snarky young woman who is the focus of the series. Her friends confide in her, but Luo Zhi herself rarely ever confides in others. She, instead, mostly keeps to herself.
Sheng Huainan is a smart and often unemotional yet caring college student who many students, other than Luo Zhi, attempt to pursue.
Zhang Mingrui is a friend of Luo Zhi and Sheng Huainan. He is often portrayed as the comic relief who struggles to find a relationship due to every person he is interested in is attracted to Sheng Huainan instead.
